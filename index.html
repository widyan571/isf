WHAT IS ISF ?

Indonesia Scholarship Festival is an exhibition of scholarship and post graduate that presenting 20 scholarship providers, more than 50 best domestic and international universities, institution, government, and Indonesian innovative companies. This event presented by Koperasi Cempaka and will be the spectacular education exhibition in Indonesia.

OUR VISION

To become a superior and highly competitive international scholarship and university exhibition in Indonesia.
