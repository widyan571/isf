WORKSHOP OF STUDY ABROAD

  • Our Program
  • Workshop of Study Abroad

Schofest Coaching Clinic (SCC) is held to explain about whole preparation for studying abroad, about student life and what to do after graduation. This program not only provides short-training but also advanced program to ensure that participants get sufficient capabilities to encounter either entrance selection or scholarship selection. Participants will be targeted to be prospective students and scholarship candidates in the future.
